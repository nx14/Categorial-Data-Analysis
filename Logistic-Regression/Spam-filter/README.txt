Dataset: spam.data is a collection of 4601 emails of which 1813 were considered “spam”, i.e., unsolicited commercial email. The “spam” concept is diverse: advertisements for products/websites, make money fast schemes, chain letters, pornography... The dataset consists of 58 attributes of which 57 are continuous predictors and one is a class label that indicates whether the email was considered spam (1) or not (0). 
Documentation files: spam.info and spam.names give more informations about predictors attributes.

Fitted a logistic regression model for predicting whether an email is ‘spam’ based on training data.
Reported what percentage was misclassified, and of all the non-spam emails of the test set.
Constructed a parsimonious word set for spam filtering by the traditional stepwise procedures or the l1-penalized logistic regression.
